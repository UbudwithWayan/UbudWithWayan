Discover the Real Bali

Ubud with Wayan is a personal, locally-run tour and transport service based in Ubud, Bali — offering travelers a warm, authentic gateway into the island's soul.

Led by Wayan, a trusted Balinese driver and guide with deep knowledge of the region, the experience is all about connection, comfort, and discovering the real Bali — far beyond the tourist trail.

From lush rice terraces and sacred temples to hidden waterfalls and traditional villages, Ubud with Wayan curates personalized journeys that feel like exploring with a friend — not following a script. Wayan speaks fluent English, drives with care, and treats each guest like family.

Whether you're in Bali for one day or one month, Ubud with Wayan offers the kind of experience that stays with you long after the road ends.

Wayan's Story & Values

Born and raised in a traditional village near Ubud, Wayan has an intimate understanding of Bali's rich culture, hidden gems, and local way of life. His passion is sharing the authentic beauty of his homeland with visitors, ensuring every journey is memorable, safe, and filled with genuine Balinese hospitality.

He believes in honest service, treating guests like family, and creating connections that go beyond typical tourism. With Wayan, you're not just a client; you're a friend exploring Bali together.
